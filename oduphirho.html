Personal Integrity

As a Phi Rho sister, we strive to follow our moral and ethical convictions by doing the right thing in all circumstances, even when no one is watching. This means being true to ourselves, acting with honor, and treating others with respect.

Academic Excellence

As engineers, we value our education and understand how important it is to excel in the classroom. In Phi Rho, sisters have the opportunity to study together and exchange helpful tips. Check out our list of accepted majors!

Lasting Friendships

In Phi Rho, lasting friendships are made with every sister. Bonds that are formed between meetings, socials, and those 2AM study sessions last well past graduation. After all, nobody understands the stress of engineering like another engineer!

Why Phi Sigma Rho?

Phi Sigma Rho is a social sorority for women in engineering and engineering technology. The sorority was founded in 1984 at Purdue University by two women who were unable to particiapte in traditional sorority rushes due to the demands of their engineering program. They decided to start their own sorority instead. Since the founding of the Alpha chapter, Phi Sigma Rho has grown to 39 chapters nationally.

Through Phi Sigma Rho, our sisters develop the highest standard of personal inegrity, strive for academic excellence, and build friendships that will last a lifetime. The sorority offers academic and professional excellence with a social balance. We invite you to join our sisterhood today!
